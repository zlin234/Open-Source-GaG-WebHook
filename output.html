About Grow a Garden Stock

Grow a Garden's shops cycle new items at set intervals. Our tracker logs each restock so you always know when seeds, gear, or eggs refresh.

Seed Shop

Updates every 5 minutes with a rotating seed selection—rare types show up less often and are prime for trading.

Gear Shop

Refreshes on a 5-minute cadence, stocking tools and equipment to boost harvest yields and mutation chances.

Egg Shop

Restocks every 10 minutes with eggs that hatch into pets, each offering unique perks for your garden's growth.

Cosmetics Shop

Always open beside the main vendor stalls, this stand lets you customise your plot with décor such as the Frog Fountain, Red Tractor, and even a rare Bloodmoon Crate. Perfect for turning a plain farm into a showcase.

Twilight Shop

When the Lunar Glow night begins, a glowing kiosk rises from the ground. For a few minutes you can pick up moon-themed exclusives like the Moon Cat pet, the Star Caller gear, and limited Night Seed Packs / Night Eggs before the sun returns.

Blood Moon Shop

Only during a rare Blood Moon (≈33 % chance at night) does this crimson market appear, offering high-value items such as Blood Banana seeds, the Blood Owl pet, and the sought-after Star Caller. Miss the 15-minute window and you may wait hours for another shot.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Grow a Garden stock?

Grow a Garden is a popular game on the Roblox platform where players cultivate virtual gardens, grow various plants, and can trade items. "Stock" in Grow a Garden refers to the rotating selection of items (like seeds, gear, eggs, and pets) available for purchase in the in-game shops. These shops refresh their inventory at set intervals, and all players across all servers see the same items.

This website, GrowaGardenStock.com, functions as a live stock tracker for the Grow a Garden game. It monitors the in-game shops and provides real-time updates on what items are currently available, along with countdown timers indicating when the next restock will occur. This helps players stay informed about rare or desired items without needing to constantly check the game itself.

How often do Grow a Garden shops restock?

 Seed & Gear shops refresh every 5 minutes, the Egg shop every 10 minutes, and the Pet shop every 30 minutes. Event shops have custom timers.

Is this an official Roblox website?

No. We're a fan-made tool and are not affiliated with Roblox or the Grow a Garden dev team.
